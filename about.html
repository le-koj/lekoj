Le Koj is a Huntington Beach based photographer, multimedia engineer and font-end web developer.

After graduating honor roll with a 3.6 gap, he proceeded to study and practice advance mathematics and physics at Virginia Commonwealth University for 2 years.

As a curious individual with an insatiable thirst for knowledge and understanding, he determined to educate himself on various disciplines such as quantum mechanics, economics, differential calculus, logic etc.

Feeling unsatisfied, He favored to pursue photography, film, and media engineering as a platform to amplify his creativity.

The innate nature, for a single well framed photograph to tell an entire story, well since before motion picture, has always been attractive to Le Koj.

It is this driving force, which attracts people to stare at a photograph, that motivated Le Koj to acquire his 1st Canon DSLR, the spring of 2012.

With years passed and riveting opportunities, the photography of Le Koj has spanned public events, venues, private occasions, startups etc.

Though the camera can never truly capture entirely what the eye sees, with continuous study and disciplined practice, One can ever get close to 99.999%.

The main tools involved in Le Koj photograph are Canon DSLRs and the Adobe Creative Suite.

Le Koj’s passion for programming stems from the nature of coding, in which, practically, every algorithm is a puzzle to be solved.

The thrill of being presented with new problems at every turn, and having to generate solution after solution to resolve the problem, are what empowers him daily to practice programming.

Le Koj’s 1st experience with programming began with MySQL, followed by python, HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Till date, Le Koj has knowledge of numerous languages and standards including php, jQuery, OOP etc.

As he continues to advance his knowledge, the immediate focus is the design and, development of interactive and secure front-end applications.

Le Koj completed his 1st film at the end of the summer of 2011. The subjects of the production involved his college colleagues, and the theme revolved around their most excitable and memorable exploits of the summer. a single Flip UtraHD video camera, his 1st video camera, filmed each moment. Though, not very satisfactory, it was a great lesson in video making and filming.

Le Koj has continued to chisel and strengthen his filming and video making knowledge through the practice of photography, exploring literatures on film theory and video production, and implementing practices acquired from various sources.

Since 2014, Le Koj produced for 2 Virginia based media startups, and has continued to film diverse events, venues and occasions.

All Le Koj media creations are influenced by creativity and elegance, as is evidence of the current webpage design.
